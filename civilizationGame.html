Game Instructions

Game Objective

Expand your civilization by founding cities, researching technologies, and managing resources. Defeat your enemies or achieve technological superiority to win the game.

Basic Controls

  • End Turn: Click the "End Turn" button to finish your turn and let other players (AI) take their turns.
  • Diplomacy: Click the "Diplomacy" button to view your relations with other civilizations and propose trades.
  • Technology: Click the "Technology" button to research new technologies.

Moving Units

Select a unit by clicking on it. Then, right-click on a tile to set a destination. The unit will move toward the destination if it has enough moves.

Combat

Move your units next to enemy units or cities to attack them. Units can only attack once per turn.

Diplomacy

Use the Diplomacy panel to view your relations with other civilizations. You can propose trades to improve relations or gain resources.

City Management

Click on one of your cities to manage it. You can assign production tasks, such as building units or researching technologies.

Research

Click the "Technology" button to open the Technology Tree. Select a technology to start researching it. Researching technologies unlocks new units and buildings.
