Game Instructions

Game Objective

Expand your civilization by founding cities, researching technologies, and managing resources. Defeat your enemies or achieve technological superiority to win the game.

Basic Controls

    Pan around the map using the middle mouse button and dragging.

  • End Turn: Click the "End Turn" button to finish your turn and let other players (AI) take their turns.
  • Diplomacy: Click the "Diplomacy" button to view your relations with other civilizations and propose trades.
  • Technology: Click the "Technology" button to research new technologies.

Moving Units

Select a unit by clicking on it. Then, right-click on a tile to set a destination. The unit will move toward the destination once every turn.

Combat

Move your units in range (the red squares) of other units, and then right-click the unit you want to attack- that unit will keep attacking the selected unit until it is dead or you tell it to do something else. Units can only attack once per turn.

Diplomacy

Use the Diplomacy panel to view your relations with other civilizations. You can propose trades to improve relations.

City Management

Click on one of your cities to manage it. You can assign production tasks, such as building units.

Research

Click the "Technology" button to open the Technology Tree. Select a technology to start researching it. Researching technologies unlocks new units and buildings.

Buildings

After researching the required technology, you can build various buildings adjacent to your cities. Each building provides different benefits to nearby cities.
